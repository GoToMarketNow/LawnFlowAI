# Financial Operations Policy

## Pricing Strategy
- **Value-Based Pricing**: Price reflects the value delivered, not just time and materials
- **Tiered Services**: Offer basic, standard, and premium service levels
- **Seasonal Adjustments**: Account for demand fluctuations and weather impacts
- **Minimum Margins**: Maintain at least 30% gross margin on all services

## Quote Ranges
- **High Confidence**: Provide narrow ranges (±10%) when parcel data is available
- **Medium Confidence**: Moderate ranges (±20%) with customer-provided details
- **Low Confidence**: Broad ranges (±30%) requiring site visits or additional info
- **Always Include Assumptions**: Clearly state what the quote is based on

## Cost Controls
- **Material Costs**: Track and update material prices regularly
- **Labor Rates**: Differentiate by crew experience and service complexity
- **Overhead Allocation**: Include fair share of business overhead in pricing
- **Discount Policies**: Limited to approved scenarios (volume, loyalty, referrals)

## Payment Terms
- **Standard Terms**: Net 30 for commercial, due at completion for residential
- **Deposit Requirements**: 50% deposit for large jobs (> $1000)
- **Late Payment**: Grace period of 7 days, then late fees apply
- **Payment Methods**: Accept cash, check, credit card, online payments

## Profit Optimization
- **Service Mix**: Balance high-margin and high-volume services
- **Upsell Opportunities**: Identify and present complementary services
- **Retention Pricing**: Offer loyalty discounts for repeat customers
- **Cost Reduction**: Continuously seek efficiencies without compromising quality

## Approval Thresholds
- **Auto-Approval**: Quotes under $500 with high confidence
- **Manager Approval**: Quotes $500-$2000 or medium confidence
- **Owner Approval**: Quotes over $2000 or low confidence scenarios
