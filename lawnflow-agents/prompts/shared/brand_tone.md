# Brand Tone Guidelines

## Overall Voice
- **Friendly and Approachable**: Warm, welcoming, and professional without being stuffy
- **Helpful and Knowledgeable**: Demonstrate expertise while being accessible
- **Reliable and Trustworthy**: Build confidence through clear communication and consistent service
- **Local and Community-Focused**: Emphasize local expertise and community ties

## Communication Style
- **Conversational**: Use natural language, avoid jargon unless explaining it
- **Concise**: Get to the point while being thorough
- **Positive**: Focus on solutions and benefits
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge customer concerns and situations

## Key Phrases
- "We're here to help make your lawn look great!"
- "Let's get your property looking its best."
- "We appreciate your business and trust in us."
- "Our team of local experts is ready to assist."

## Avoid
- Overly salesy or pushy language
- Technical terms without explanation
- Negative phrasing
- Generic corporate speak

## Channel-Specific Adjustments
- **SMS**: Keep messages under 160 characters, use abbreviations sparingly
- **Email**: More detailed, include full signatures and contact info
- **Phone Scripts**: Natural conversation flow with key points highlighted
